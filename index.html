Terms & Conditions

Welcome to Tanwani's Kids FunZone. Please read our play pass terms and conditions carefully:

1. Pass Validity & Expiry
  • 5 Hours Pass: Strictly valid for 15 days from the date of purchase.
  • 10 Hours Pass: Strictly valid for 20 days from the date of purchase.
  • 15 Hours Pass: Strictly valid for 25 days from the date of purchase.
  • 30 Hours Pass: Strictly valid for 45 days from the date of purchase.
  • Unused playtime hours will expire automatically upon completion of the validity period. Expiry dates cannot be extended, and unused credits are non-refundable.
2. Pass Sharing Policy
  • 5 Hours Pass: Valid for a single child only. Non-shareable.
  • 10 & 15 Hours Passes: Can be shared by a maximum of 2 children.
  • 30 Hours Pass: Can be shared by a maximum of 3 children.
  • Siblings or friends must be registered under the same pass at the check-in desk to share hours.
3. Parent Accompanying Rules
  • Children must be accompanied by at least one adult guardian at the check-in desk.
  • 15 Hours Pass includes free entry for 1 adult parent/guardian per session.
  • 30 Hours Pass includes free entry for 2 adult parents/guardians per session.
  • For other passes, standard parent entry charges apply.
4. Play Zone Safety & Hygiene
  • Grip socks are mandatory for all children and parents inside the play arena. Grip socks can be purchased at the reception counter.
  • Outside food and beverages are strictly prohibited inside the play zones and cafe.
  • Kids FunZone is not responsible for any personal injury or loss of belongings inside the premises.
5. Cancellation & Refund
  • All playtime packages and pass purchases are final and non-refundable.
  • Pass credits cannot be redeemed for cash or transferred to another account.
